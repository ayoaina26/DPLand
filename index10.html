About Us

"The legendary and all encompassing free resource!" - Ayo Aina

Hello and welcome to DPLand! An online tutoring and revision hub for all things IB Diploma Program. It also allows you to access tutoring from other DP students in our Discord, as well as to share your experiences with other students, maximising your exam revision before the big test day in May or November. Our goal is to democratize knowledge for all, an especially our comrades in the DP program of IB. In this regard, systematically making our website accessible is our ultimate goal.
